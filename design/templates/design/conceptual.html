    Scrolling

    An example of where conceptual models come into play is when scrolling on devices. Scrolling is an almost automatic reaction, and they way in which we scroll largely depends on the device you are using. If you have a smart phone you most likely are used to scrolling with your finger. The actions that you perform are slightly different from what you would on a PC. Let's check it out by playing a little game. The aim of the game is to press the STOP button as quickly as you can. You have to scroll to find it though! Press begin to start.
