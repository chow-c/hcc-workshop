    Using the Driving Simulator

    In this activity, you will be able to play with the driving simulator.

    There is only one driving simulator available, so if it is already occupied, you may need to attempt another activity. Alternatively, you can go watch your friends and cheer them on!

    Pre-race Checklist

    Have you ever driven a real car?

    Controls

    Familiarise yourself with the controls.

    HCC Crash Course in Driving

    1. Engage the brake

      Place your foot firmly on the brake pedal. Do not remove it until step 5.

    2. Start the car

      Start the ignition by pushing the ignition button once. The ignition icon will disappear from the dashboard onscreen.

    3. Remove the handbrake

      Take off the handbrake by pressing the handbrake button once. The handbrake icon will disappear from the dashboard onscreen.

    4. Put the car into gear

      We are driving an automatic. Tap the gear knob down toward you three times to move from Park (P) to Drive (D). The gear display onscreen will change.

    5. Drive

      Release the brake pedal, engage the accelerator and steer as required.

    Activity: Test Drive

    You're all set! Take the car for a leisurely drive, but try not to hurt too many pedestrians.

    Be courteous

    In the interests of fairness, be kind to your colleagues and don't spend too much time on the simulator.
