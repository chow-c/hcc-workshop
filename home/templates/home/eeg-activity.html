    Using the Emotiv Insight

    In this activity, you will be able to use an Emotiv Insight on yourself in order to visualise your EEG activity.

    Initial Setup

    You will need:

    • An iPad (Hint: Use the iPad mini with the green cover!)
    • The Emotiv Insight
    These devices will be available at the front of the room.

    There is only one of these devices available, so if it is not free, you may need to attempt another activity in the meantime.

    You can complete this activity individually. You can also work with your colleagues in order to compare how your brain wave activity might be different to theirs.

    Putting the Insight on

    1. Place the Insight on your head

      The large bulky part should sit above your left ear. The small part that extends underneath the Emotiv logo sits on the bone just behind your ear.

    2. Adjust the front electrodes

      The two front electrodes should be approximately three finger-widths above your eyes.

    Pairing the devices

    Once the Emotiv is on your head, you need to pair the device to the iPad.

    1. Launch the MyEmotiv application

      Launch the application by touching the MyEmotiv icon on the iPad. It should be on the third home screen.

    2. Prepare the E4

      Make sure the Emotiv Insight is turned on. Press the button near the logo.

    3. Start A New Session

      In the application, press the blinking black question mark icon and select the available headset. You may need to select the icon again to calibrate the device.

    4. Off you go!

      Once you have a reasonable contact quality (greater than 85% should do it), hit Begin to start visualising.

    Activity: Control your brain activity

    Visualise your brain activity while thinking about different things. Do you notice any difference in the activations?.

    Cleaning Up

    Once you've had enough, take the Insight off and press the button on the device to turn it off. Turn off the iPad screen for the next person.

    Be courteous

    Once finished with this activity, please return the iPad and Emotiv Insight device to the front for your colleagues to try.
