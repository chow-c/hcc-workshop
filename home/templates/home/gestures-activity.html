    Using the Myo Armband

    In this activity, you will be able to use the Myo Armband to perform gesture commands on a PowerPoint presentation.

    Initial Setup

    The Myo Armband will only work with the laptop to which it is connected via a Bluetooth dongle. Please approach the Myo Armband workstation to try this activity.

    There is only one Myo Armband available, so if it is already occupied, you may need to attempt another activity. Alternatively, you can watch your friends and cheer them on!

    Activity: Calibration

    Like many devices monitoring user behaviour, the Myo Armband needs to be calibrated for every person to enable it to work at its best. Can you think of why this might be the case? Hint.

    Launch the Myo Armband calibration sequence. Work through the instructions to calibrate the Myo to recognise your gestures and body movements.

    Activity: Gesture Control in a PowerPoint Presentation

    Now that you are calibrated, switch over to the open PowerPoint presentation in order to try out the gestures.

    Be courteous

    In the interests of fairness, be kind to your colleagues and don't spend too much time using the Myo Armband.
