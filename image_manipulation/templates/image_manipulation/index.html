    Can you pick which images have been manipulated?

    One of the areas of interest to the HCC group is in image manipulation. The central question of this research is, can people tell if an image has been manipulated or not? Now we've all seen images that have clearly been tampered with, and not very well, but there are others out there that are photoshoped and and you can't obvious detect the change.

    In this activity you will be shown nine images and asked whether the image has been manipulated or not. At the end, you'll get to see how well you did.
